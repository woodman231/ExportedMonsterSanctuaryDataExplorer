Critical Hybridization

This page lists details for the Critical Hybridization skill in the Monster Sanctuary video game.

Name
Critical Hybridization
Description (Some descriptions may vary depending on the specific monster that has the skill)
The first critical hit of a physical attack triggers an additional hit equal to 60% of Magic value and vice versa.
