Cascade

This page lists details for the Cascade skill in the Monster Sanctuary video game.

Name
Cascade
Description (Some descriptions may vary depending on the specific monster that has the skill)
The power of every consecutive hit during an ability is increased by 5% continuously. (For a 4 hit attack: +0%, +5%, +10%, +15%)
